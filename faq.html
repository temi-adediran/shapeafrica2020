Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Shape Africa?
A: Shape Africa is an annual Global Shapers Community event that brings together Global shapers from around Africa and the world to a major city in Africa (physically or virtually) to discuss topics and pursue solutions that concern the wellbeing of the African continent.

Q: When and where is Shape Africa 2021?
A: Shape Africa 2021 will be held in Lagos, Nigeria from 9 - 11 December. Hosted by the Global Shapers Community, Lagos hub.

Q: How can I participate in Shape Africa?

Q: You said if I meet the requirements for SHAPE, what are the requirements?
A: To attend Shape Africa 2021 physically in Lagos, here are the mandatory requirements:

  • Link to Toplink profile as proof of GSC membership
  • Must reside in Nigeria
  • Proof of COVID-19 vaccination
  • Negative COVID-19 test
To attend Shape Africa 2021 virtually you only need to register with link to Toplink profile as proof of GSC membership.

Q: Is Shape Africa 2021 holding virtually or physically?
A: Due to COVID-19 pandemic restrictions and WEF HQ guidelines, Shape Africa 2021 is going to be a hybrid event hosted in Lagos Nigeria and streamed globally. Only shapers resident in Nigeria will be present physically with other shapers from Africa and the world joining virtually.

Q: I am not from Africa, but I'll be in Nigeria around the dates for SHAPE. Can I still attend?
A: If you meet the 3 requirements above, yes you can.
