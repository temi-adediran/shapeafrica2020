Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I need to pay a commitment fee?
A: The non-refundable commitment fee is to guarantee and confirm your seat at SHAPE Africa 2020. Only participants that don't require a visa to Nigeria are required to pay the commitment fee.

Q: How do I obtain a Visa for Nigeria?
A: The entire visa process has been captured and explained on the visa page here. If you require further assistance please send an email to visa@shapeafrica2020.com

Q: Do I need to pay a commitment fee before or after obtaining a visa?
A: All intending shape attendees who require a visa to attend do not need to pay an initial commitment before you apply for a Visa. However, there is a visa confirmation deadline.

Q: What happens if I am unable to get a visa from my home country?
A: If you are unable to obtain a visa before the application deadline not to worry, kindly email visa@shapeafrica2020.com and we’ll walk you through assisting you through the visa on arrival process after commitment fees have been paid.

Q: How will I receive my visa letter of invitation?
A: As soon as you indicate interest for a visa and submit your details you will receive a signed copy of an invitation letter no later than 5 working days after.

Q: What hotel should I stay at during Shape Africa?
A: Hotel information will be sent to you after confirmation of attendance via obtained visa/commitment fee. A block of rooms has been reserved at a special rate and will be filled on a first-come, first-served basis. Please contact the hotel directly; remember to make your room reservations early and mention the Global Shapers Community.

Q: What meals will be served at Shape Africa 2020?
A: Continental breakfast is provided each morning prior to the scheduled start time either by the hotel or the Lagos Hub. There will be morning and afternoon refreshment breaks for full-day sessions, and lunch will be provided for full-day sessions or where expressly indicated.​ If you have dietary preferences, please be sure to state those on the space provided on the registration form when you register.

Q: What services are available for special needs and requests?
A: We encourage persons with special needs to attend and participate in Shape 2020. Please contact hello@shapeafrica2020.com to request an interpreter services or other accommodations for special needs. If you have dietary preferences, please be sure to state those on the space provided on the registration form when you register.

Q: In which city in Lagos is the main venue for SHAPE Africa 2020 located?
A: Victoria Island
