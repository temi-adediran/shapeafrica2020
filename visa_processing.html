Pre-departure formalities

Vaccination

You will need to see your doctor to get vaccinated for Yellow Fever, which is required for entry into Nigeria.

This should be done a minimum of 10days before your departure date. Also make sure you are given a “Yellow Card” after vaccination”. You should have this on you on arrival at Murtala Muhammed International Airport, Lagos

The best way to go about it is to see a doctor or a travel clinic in your locality and say you will be visiting Nigeria and they will provide you with the necessary vaccines.

You should also discuss Malaria Prophylaxis with your doctor, there are a couple of options if you choose to take anti-malaria drugs.

Business Visa and Visa On Arrival Application

If you have a passport which requires its holders to obtain a visa before coming to Nigeria, you will need to make sure you start your visa application process as soon as possible.

Business Visa

We advise that all intending shape attendees apply for a business no more than 6-weeks to your arrival date.

Business Visa is available to citizens of all countries except ECOWAS Nationals who do not require a visa to visit Nigeria and other countries which Nigeria has entered into visa abolition agreements.

Here is a quick list to guide you through this step:

  1. Complete the registration form and select your visa preference(If you haven’t already)
  2. Wait to receive a copy of an Invitation Letter from The Lagos Hub.
    • If you require additional documentation or support, do not hesitate to send an email to visa@shapeafrica2021.com with the subject line: “Additional Documentation for Visa Processing”.
  3. Once you’ve received an invitation letter please begin your business visa application here. If you are unsure about the visa costs for your nationality please find costs here. Visa On Arrival (VOA)

If you are planning on arriving on the Visa On Arrival Program, Please note that you can only complete a Visa on Arrival Application at the earliest two weeks to your date of arrival.

Here is a quick list to guide you through this step:

  1. Complete the registration form and select your visa preference (If you haven’t already)
  2. Wait to receive a copy of an /Invitation Letter from and other supporting documents form the Lagos Hub. (find out more about the Visa on Arrival Program here).
    • If you require additional documentation or support, do not hesitate to send an email to visa@shapeafrica2020.com with the subject line: “Additional Documentation for Visa Processing”.
  3. Once you’ve received all the support please begin your VOA application here. If you are unsure about the visa costs for your nationality please find costs here.
Please note these fees go directly to the Nigerian immigration and is in no way connected to Shape Africa 2020

Safety and Security in Lagos

We are providing adequate security for your stay all through SHAPE Africa. Like in any city, you should be aware while you are walking or commuting in any form of transportation, particularly at night. Being aware of one’s surroundings and oneself is important and we would recommend avoiding moving alone at night.

Any safety concerns should be brought immediately to the logistics team at hello@shapeafrica2020.com.
